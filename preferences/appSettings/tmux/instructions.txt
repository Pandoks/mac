# Instructions to follow for a successful installation of Pandoks_'s tmux settings

After exiting these instructions, you will be brought into tmux.
You MUST follow these instructions for a successful installation of Pandoks_'s tmux settings

1. Wait for installations of plugins (look at yellow bar at bottom of terminal)
2. Wait for status to say "Tmux resurrect file not found!"
3. Type ctrl+"a", then "I"
4. Press ENTER/ESCAPE or follow instructions to continue
5. Type "exit"

Type ":q" to quit
